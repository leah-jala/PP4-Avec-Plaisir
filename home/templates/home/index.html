Avec Plaisir

"Avec Plaisir" means "with pleasure"! We welcome you and prepare your meal with great enthusiasm. We've been serving classic French dishes since 2005, using only the freshest seasonal produce. The food may be old school, but our environment is lively and relaxed. We've been repeatedly reviewed as one of the best restaurants in London that won't break your wallet.
